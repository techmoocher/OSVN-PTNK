Changing Lives one Smile at a time

Founded at VNUHCM High School for the Gifted, Operation Smile @ PTNK mobilises students to fundraise, volunteer, and advocate for children born with cleft lip and palate. We partner with Operation Smile Vietnam to support medical missions and post-operation care.

What we do

Operation Smile PTNK volunteers coordinate year-round activities that combine fundraising, advocacy, and hands-on service to support medical missions across Vietnam.

  • Host charity concerts, bake sales, and campus campaigns that fund surgeries.
  • Coordinate volunteer delegations for Operation Smile missions and patient support days.
  • Lead awareness workshops for students and parents about cleft lip and palate care.
  • Partner with local hospitals and alumni to deliver post-operation care packages.
