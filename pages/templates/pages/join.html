Join Operation Smile PTNK

Become part of a student-led movement that funds cleft surgeries, partners with Operation Smile Vietnam, and brings compassion to every mission. We welcome students with every skillset—from event planning and communications to logistics and design.

Complete the Google Form Email the leadership team

Why students join

  • Plan creative fundraisers that directly support medical missions.
  • Volunteer alongside doctors and nurses on Operation Smile trips.
  • Develop leadership, project management, and teamwork skills.
  • Make lifelong friendships with passionate student volunteers.

How to get started

  1. Fill out the Google Form so we know your interests and availability.
  2. Join our next general meeting to meet the leadership team.
  3. Choose a committee or project that fits your strengths.
  4. Stay connected via email and Instagram for updates.

Questions?

DM us on Instagram @operationsmileptnk or send an email—our student leaders are ready to help.
